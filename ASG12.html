About

NewJeans is a South Korean girl group formed by ADOR, a subsidiary of HYBE Labels. The group consists of five members: Minji, Hanni, Danielle, Haerin, and Hyein. They debuted on July 22, 2022, with the single “Attention” and released their self-titled debut extended play on August 1, 2022. NewJeans is known for their fresh image and music that blends pop and R&B with influences from various dance and club styles. Each member brings their unique background and talents to the group: Minji is the group’s rapper, known for her energetic performances and love for journaling. Hanni is a vocalist with Vietnamese-Australian heritage, adding a multicultural flair to the group. Danielle contributes her vocal skills and has a background in television and dance. Haerin and Hyein round out the group with their vocal talents and youthful energy. NewJeans has quickly gained popularity for their catchy tunes and relatable lyrics, appealing to a wide audience both in South Korea and internationally
